Ghost Hunters

Roles: Designer, Writer, Tester.
A cooperative mystery board game where players explore a haunted manor, solve clues, and exorcise ghosts using custom logic-based mechanics. Inspired by classic deduction games with a spooky twist.

Backstabbing Bastards

Roles: Concept, Mechanics, Rulebook.
A competitive game where alliances form and break in a race to survive a collapsing bunker. Lies, betrayal, and social deduction are key.

Bermudes à la mort

Roles: Lead Designer, Illustrator.
A survival game set in the Bermuda Triangle. Players manage resources, navigate storms, and sabotage each other’s plans to escape.
