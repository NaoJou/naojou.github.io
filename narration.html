Leoncitoze and the Standing Warriors

View Game Design Document (PDF)

A game design document for my upcoming project: a narrative-driven point-and-click adventure where you play as Leoncitoze, striving to save your people from the oppressive Standing Warriors and their chief, Dollarus II. The game explores themes of colonization, cultural identity, resistance, and freedom. The project is currently in development — and evolving every day!

Veil of the dreambound

Link coming soon

I co-designed the narrative for Veil of the Dreambound alongside Elise Warren. Together, we crafted the entire story arc of the game. Our responsibilities included writing all character dialogues, building the overarching lore, and creating in-world content. We also connected the bedroom’s elements with the recurring nightmare sequences to ensure narrative coherence. Drawing on my doctoral background in trauma studies, I designed nightmares that reflect the psychological mechanisms of PTSD and traumatic memory. It was a challenging process, but above all, an incredibly fun experience.
